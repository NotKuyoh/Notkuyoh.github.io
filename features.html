Detailed Overview of JOI™ Features

Explore the powerful capabilities of JOI™ that transform your home into a truly smart environment.

JOI™ is

innovative. intelligent. efficient. adaptive. reliable.

Intelligent Python Intent Recognition

JOI™ leverages advanced Python-based models to recognize and interpret your commands with high accuracy. With intent recognition, JOI™ determines the exact action you want, enhancing responsiveness and efficiency.

Real-Time Conversation Processing

With the powerful Ollama AI model, JOI™ processes conversations in real-time, creating smooth, engaging interactions. JOI™ can answer questions, adjust settings, and complete tasks as naturally as if you were conversing with a person.

Energy Management System

JOI™ optimizes energy usage across all devices, promoting a greener lifestyle. By managing power consumption, JOI™ helps reduce utility bills while maintaining device performance and efficiency.

Smart Security Monitoring

Integrated with smart security systems, JOI™ provides real-time monitoring and automatic alerts, enhancing home safety. This feature responds to potential security threats, giving you peace of mind.

Automated Device Control

JOI™ can seamlessly control all connected devices, from adjusting lighting to setting thermostats. This automation reduces the need for manual adjustments, letting JOI™ handle routine tasks.

Customizable Scene Settings

Create personalized "scenes" for various times or events, such as "Movie Night" or "Morning Routine." JOI™ tailors your home’s atmosphere with a single command.
